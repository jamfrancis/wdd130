Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a good trip for kids? What are the age ranges you allow?

Yes, this trip is suitable for kids of all ages. While children over 8 tend to enjoy it more, boredom is rarely an issue. The stunning scenery and exciting rapids keep everyone engaged. Our experienced guides share entertaining stories, making the journey fascinating for all. Parents and children alike find this adventure unforgettable!

Is whitewater rafting safe? I don't even know how to swim! Can I still come?

As with all adventure sports, there is an inherent and assumed element of risk. To help ensure your safety, our guides are all certified in Wilderness First aid and all start with at least 3 years of rafting experience. The most common injuries are sunburns and injuries that occur on land. Everyone on commercial rafting trips wears coast guard approved life vest or personal flotation device (PFD). Many non swimmers go on rafting trips and have a great time. Your guide will give you an in depth and engaging safety talk before your trip begins explaining what you should prepare for and do in the event that you fall out of the raft.

What is included in the trip?

We will provide all necessary camping equipment. This includes a sleeping bag, foam pad, waterproof duffle bag, small waterproof day bag, tent and a cot, eating utensils and camping chair.

What do I need to bring?

Gear up for an adrenaline-pumping white water rafting adventure! Pack smart with versatile clothing for scorching heat and chilly waters: think rain gear, quick-dry shirts, and shorts. Don't miss out on essentials like a stylish hat, adventure-ready sunglasses, and comfy socks. Strap on sturdy sandals or water shoes and grab a sleek day pack. Pack personal must-haves, sunscreen, and a trusty water bottle. Optional extras? Think gloves, a cozy fleece jacket, and a camera to capture those epic moments. Get ready to conquer the rapids in style!

What is so great about whitewater rafting anyway?

Whitewater rafting is an exhilarating adventure that offers unforgettable experiences! Picture waking up under a starlit sky, followed by a hearty breakfast. Days are filled with thrilling rapids, scenic hikes, fascinating history, and delicious meals. In the evening, relax by the river with a book or good company, savoring a delectable dinner prepared by guides, perhaps steak or grilled salmon, and ending the night with stories under a canopy of stars. It's a journey you won't want to miss!

How far in advance should I book my trip?

If you have specific dates you need, we recommend booking a year in advance. If you can be more flexible 6 months in advance is usually sufficient.
