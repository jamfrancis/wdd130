Projects

Paddle Power

Among these endeavors stands out my work on the Paddle Power White Water Rafting site, a project that encapsulates my dedication to crafting engaging online experiences. Through meticulous design and innovative user interfaces, I transformed the Paddle Power website into a digital gateway for adventure enthusiasts. Navigating the thrilling waters of creativity, I integrated dynamic visuals, intuitive navigation, and seamless functionality, ensuring visitors could effortlessly explore the excitement and adrenaline of white water rafting. This project not only challenged my technical skills but also allowed me to merge my love for outdoor activities with my expertise in web development, resulting in a vibrant, user-friendly platform that captures the essence of the exhilarating rafting experience. Explore this project and witness how my passion for design meets the rush of the river, creating a seamless harmony in the online realm.

Retail Sales Calculator

Dive into one of my intriguing projects, the Retail Sales Calculator, where I combined my passion for programming with practical problem-solving skills. This web application stands as a testament to my proficiency in web development. The program takes customer subtotal as input and processes the total amount based on specific conditions. If the subtotal exceeds $50 and the day falls on Tuesday or Wednesday, a 10% discount is applied, followed by a 6% sales tax addition. For other days of the week or subtotals less than $50, only the 6% sales tax is added. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for efficient coding, I created an interactive interface where users can input their subtotal and instantly calculate the adjusted price. This project showcases my ability to transform complex retail calculations into a seamless digital experience, highlighting my expertise in both frontend and backend web development. Explore this project to witness how I turn intricate retail logic into a user-friendly application, bridging the gap between intricate algorithms and effortless user interaction.
